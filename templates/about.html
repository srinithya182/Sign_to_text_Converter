About Us

Meet the team behind the Indian Sign Language Recognition project

Our Mission

At ISL Recognition, we are committed to breaking down communication barriers between the deaf community and the hearing world. Our mission is to leverage artificial intelligence and computer vision to create accessible tools that facilitate seamless communication.

With over 70 million people in India using sign language, our goal is to make everyday interactions more inclusive and accessible for everyone.

Project Overview

Our Indian Sign Language Recognition system uses deep learning to translate sign language gestures into text. The technology is built on a powerful LSTM neural network architecture, trained on a comprehensive dataset of Indian Sign Language gestures.

The system can recognize alphabets, numbers, and common words, providing real-time translations with high accuracy.
